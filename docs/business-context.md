
# Business Context: Siena AI

## 1. Company Overview

**Siena AI** is an **AI-native customer experience (CX) platform designed for commerce brands**. The company builds autonomous AI agents that manage customer interactions across multiple channels, allowing businesses to scale support, sales, and insights without increasing headcount. ([gladly.ai][1])

The platform blends **human-like empathy with generative AI reasoning** to handle complex customer service workflows rather than simply deflecting tickets like traditional chatbots. ([siena.cx][2])

Its mission is to **make exceptional customer experiences universally accessible while unlocking human potential through automation**. ([cellpower.uz][3])

---

# 2. Core Product

### Autonomous AI Customer Service Agent

Siena provides **agentic AI that operates like a full customer support representative**, capable of understanding context and executing workflows.

Typical tasks include:

* Responding to customer inquiries
* Processing returns and exchanges
* Tracking orders
* Handling billing and subscription changes
* Providing product recommendations
* Resolving support tickets end-to-end

Unlike rule-based chatbots, Siena can **reason about complex customer queries and resolve them autonomously**. ([siena.cx][2])

---

# 3. Key Value Proposition

Siena’s primary value is **automating large volumes of customer service interactions while maintaining a human-like tone and brand consistency**.

Typical benefits for companies:

* Automates up to **80% of customer interactions** ([siena.cx][4])
* **90% faster resolution times** ([LinkedIn][5])
* **98% faster response times** ([LinkedIn][5])
* **~94.7% customer satisfaction (CSAT)** ([LinkedIn][5])

This allows companies to:

* Reduce support costs
* Improve response speed
* increase retention and revenue
* scale support without hiring more agents.

---

# 5. Core Product Capabilities

### 1. Omnichannel Customer Support

Siena can manage interactions across:

* Email
* SMS
* Social media messages
* WhatsApp
* Website chat
* Comments and DMs

All conversations are handled through a unified AI layer. ([AI Agent Store][7])

---

### 2. Workflow Automation

The platform can execute real operational tasks:

Examples:

* Process returns
* Cancel or edit orders
* Track shipping
* Update addresses
* manage subscriptions

These are handled automatically by integrating with ecommerce systems. ([Wellfound][8])

---

### 3. Shopping / Sales Agent

Siena can also function as a **shopping assistant**, helping customers:

* find products
* answer pre-purchase questions
* overcome purchase hesitation

This helps convert conversations into revenue. ([siena.cx][9])

---

### 4. Brand Intelligence Layer

Siena builds an **intelligence layer from customer interactions**, which enables:

* deep personalization
* insights into customer behavior
* knowledge sharing across teams
* continuous improvement of the AI agent

The system learns from past interactions, documentation, and team knowledge. ([siena.cx][10])

---

# 6. Technology Approach

Siena positions itself as an **AI-native CX platform**, not a chatbot add-on to existing helpdesks. ([siena.cx][11])

Core design principles include:

* Contextual reasoning instead of scripted replies
* Empathic conversation design
* Continuous learning from interactions
* integration into existing tools

This allows the AI to **solve customer issues rather than simply routing them to humans**. ([siena.cx][2])

# 8. Pricing Model

Pricing is **customized based on ticket volume and team size**. ([siena.cx][13])

Historically, the company has experimented with **per-conversation pricing**, where businesses pay a fixed amount for each conversation handled by the AI. ([siena.cx][14])
